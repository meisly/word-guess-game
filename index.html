Welcome to 9 Lives

Can you help Smoosh find their way home? Smoosh just moved to a new city and has gone exploring. Now they can't remember the way home. Smoosh remembers that there was a word written on the building they live in. Can you help Smoosh remember the word? If you guess wrong too many times Smoosh will get too far from home and will never make it back.

9 Lives

Help Smoosh find their way home by figuring our the signs on the landmarks they passed. To guess a letter just press it on the keyboard. You may press any key to begin.
